Artificial Intelligence

Our software is designed to be your sidekick in evaluating candidates. We have conducted a study in what makes a good interview, and incorporated those results into our evaluation intelligence.

The more users we have on EmployEDU, the better our scoring algorithm gets. Based on how you score candidates, our algorithm adjusts to meet your unique criteria to give you the best results possible.
